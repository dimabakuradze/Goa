In the mid-2000s, Apple, under the leadership of CEO Steve Jobs, was already a major player in the tech industry with its successful line of Macintosh computers and the iconic iPod music player. However, Jobs envisioned a revolutionary device that would combine the functionality of a phone, a music player, and an internet communicator into one sleek device.


Steve jobs

Steve Jobs, along with Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne, co-founded Apple Computer, Inc. in 1976 in the Jobs family garage. Their goal was to develop and sell personal computers.

Steve Jobs passed away on October 5, 2011, after a long battle with pancreatic cancer. His legacy lives on through Apple's continued innovation, as well as through the countless lives he touched with his visionary ideas and passion for excellence.
